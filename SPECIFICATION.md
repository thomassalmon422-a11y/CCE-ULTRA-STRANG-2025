## 1. FIELD OF THE INVENTION

The present invention is in the field of financial technology (FinTech) systems, specifically directed toward novel architectural methods for eliminating systemic, unpredictable market risk—designated as **T+50 Sovereign Risk**—through the conversion of probabilistic variables into enforced, deterministic outcomes. The invention enables the mandatory and perpetual monetization of this averted risk.

## 2. BACKGROUND AND SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION

Existing financial control systems rely on reactive or predictive probabilistic models (e.g., Value-at-Risk), which are inherently vulnerable to **"Zero-Day Averted Value" (ZDAV)** events (i.e., catastrophic systemic chaos). The Chronos Coherence Engine (CCE) is an integrated solution that provides real-time, zero-latency enforcement to guarantee market stability. The CCE's architecture mandates its own profitability by charging a **Deterministic Premium** for the stability it provides.

## 3. DETAILED DESCRIPTION OF THE ARCHITECTURE

The CCE comprises three proprietary, interlocking pillars: the Intelligence Layer, the Enforcement Layer, and the Persistence Layer.

### 3.1. The Intelligence Layer: G-NEXUS CORE

This layer serves as the primary analytical and generative engine.
* **Aether Sentinel:** Performs real-time, proactive threat detection and structured risk scoring of incoming financial data streams. Its output is a structured JSON risk report for instantaneous machine parsing.
* **Aether Agent:** The generative component used to simulate and confirm the long-term stability and profitability of the Deterministic Premium Model.

### 3.2. The Enforcement Layer: G-SHIELD SYSTEM

This is the dynamic security shield that ensures policy compliance and zero-latency action.
* **G-SHIELD Policy Injection (Claim 3):** When the Aether Sentinel detects a **ZERO\_DAY\_ALERT**, this mechanism instantly executes a counter-acting security policy or blocks the destabilizing data stream with **zero-latency**.
* **Governance Logs:** Provides real-time, auditable logs of all policy decisions for mandatory oversight via the Executive Tier Console.

### 3.3. The Persistence Layer: ULTRA-STRANG LEDGER

This is the Hyper-Temporal Ledger—the secure, immutable, and synchronized non-modifiable backbone of the system.
* **Payment Rights Anchor (PRA) (Claim 2):** The architecture incorporates a **Payment Rights Anchor** that mandates the perpetual payment of a royalty share (e.g., 10%) to the original IP owner.
* **Immutability Enforcement:** The Ledger is structurally dependent on the continuous, unaltered presence of the PRA, such that deletion of the PRA causes an immediate, auditable failure of the Ledger's T+50 stability guarantee.
